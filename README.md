# PhD Thesis

## Title: New Approaches in Mortality Modelling and Forecasting
## Author: Ugofilippo Basellini
## Defence date: 24 February 2020

This repository contains the dissertation that I have developed and written during my three-year appointment as a joint Ph.D. student at the [Institut national d'études démographiques (INED)](https://www.ined.fr/) and at the [Interdisciplinary Centre on Population Dynamics (CPop)](https://www.sdu.dk/en/forskning/forskningsenheder/samf/cpop) in the Department of Public Health of the [University of Southern Denmark](https://www.sdu.dk/en/).

In the folder presentation, you can find the slides that I have used during my PhD assessment.
